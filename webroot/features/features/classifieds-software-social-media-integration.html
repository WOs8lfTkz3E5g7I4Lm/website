Social Media Integrations

Clients Can Display Their Twitter Feeds
Your clients can insert their own twitter feed within their own listings to further leverage social media integration. If your clients do not have a twitter feed your site feed can be used instead automatically.
Facebook Register/Login
Allow users to register account using their facebook login. Their account is created using the public information that Facebook provides in the process. Client enters no information to register or login.
Push All Listings to Twitter
Using the RSS feed feature built into our software you can have all or some of your listings pushed through your sites twitter feed.
Browsers Can Share Listings Through Social Media
Any client browsing your site can share any listing they see through their social media accounts spreading the reach of your site way beyond your domain.
Push All Listings to Facebook
Using the RSS feed feature built into our software you can have all or some of your listings pushed through your sites Facebook timeline.
Sellers Can Share Listings Through Social Media
Seller can share their classifieds or auctions through their social media accounts. Share directly with facebook, twitter, reddit, and others with a click or two.
